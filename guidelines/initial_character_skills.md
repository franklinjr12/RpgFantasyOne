* Each class gets **4 skills**
* Every skill has a **clear tactical role**
* All classes are **self-sufficient**
* No skill invalidates positioning or basic attacks
* Cooldowns > mana spam (keeps combat readable)

I’ll also explain **why each skill exists**, which is more important than numbers.

---

# Global Skill Design Rules (Applies to All)

Before the skills themselves, lock these rules:

* Max **4 active skills per class**
* Cooldowns between **6–18 seconds**
* Skills *enhance* the core loop, not replace it
* No invulnerability
* Sustain exists, but requires engagement

---

# 🗡️ MELEE CLASS — “The Anchor”

**Fantasy:** Stand your ground, manage positioning, survive pressure
**Weakness:** Limited reach, punished by poor timing
**Strength:** Consistency, sustain through risk

---

## 1. Power Strike

**Type:** Active – Melee burst
**Cooldown:** Short (6–8s)

**Effect**

* Next basic attack deals bonus damage
* Slight knockback

**Why it exists**

* Teaches timing
* Gives melee a way to *end fights faster*
* Rewards aggression without becoming spam

**RO DNA:** Bash

---

## 2. Guard Stance

**Type:** Active – Defensive toggle
**Cooldown:** Medium (12s)

**Effect**

* Reduces incoming damage for a few seconds
* Movement speed reduced while active

**Why it exists**

* Lets melee survive bad pulls
* Forces a decision: tank vs reposition
* Reinforces “anchor” identity

**RO DNA:** Endure / Provoke-style tradeoff

---

## 3. Blood Oath

**Type:** Active – Sustain
**Cooldown:** Medium (10–14s)

**Effect**

* For a short duration:

  * A percentage of damage dealt heals the player

**Why it exists**

* Sustain tied to *doing damage*
* Encourages staying in melee range
* Prevents passive regen abuse

**RO DNA:** Lifesteal builds without pot spam

---

## 4. Shockwave Slam

**Type:** Active – Area control
**Cooldown:** Long (16–18s)

**Effect**

* Ground slam in small radius
* Damages and briefly slows enemies

**Why it exists**

* Crowd control without stun-locking
* Emergency button for being surrounded
* Synergizes with Guard Stance & Blood Oath

**RO DNA:** Magnum Break (minus fire abuse)

---

# 🏹 RANGED CLASS — “The Kiter”

**Fantasy:** Control space, punish approach
**Weakness:** Fragile if cornered
**Strength:** Safety through movement and spacing

---

## 1. Quick Shot

**Type:** Active – DPS boost
**Cooldown:** Short (6s)

**Effect**

* Instantly fires an extra shot
* Can crit or apply on-hit effects

**Why it exists**

* Keeps ranged combat active
* Smooth damage curve without burst dominance

**RO DNA:** Double Strafe

---

## 2. Retreat Roll

**Type:** Active – Mobility
**Cooldown:** Medium (10–12s)

**Effect**

* Quick backward roll
* Brief movement speed bonus after roll

**Why it exists**

* Gives ranged a *panic button*
* Encourages active positioning
* Prevents melee enemies from trivializing the class

**RO DNA:** Backstep-style kiting

---

## 3. Focused Aim

**Type:** Active – Precision mode
**Cooldown:** Medium (12s)

**Effect**

* Temporarily:

  * Increased damage
  * Reduced movement speed

**Why it exists**

* Risk–reward skill
* Forces player to commit to position
* Makes bosses more interesting

**RO DNA:** Sniper focus fantasy

---

## 4. Poison Tip

**Type:** Active – Damage over time
**Cooldown:** Long (16s)

**Effect**

* Next hits apply poison
* Poison deals % HP damage over time

**Why it exists**

* Helps against high-HP enemies
* Scales well without raw DPS inflation
* Encourages hit-and-run play

**RO DNA:** Assassin-style status play, adapted to ranged

---

# 🔮 CASTER CLASS — “The Controller”

**Fantasy:** High impact, careful resource management
**Weakness:** Setup time, fragile
**Strength:** Area damage, battlefield control

---

## 1. Arcane Bolt

**Type:** Active – Burst nuke
**Cooldown:** Short (6–8s)

**Effect**

* High magic damage projectile
* Slight travel time

**Why it exists**

* Burst option for priority targets
* Teaches spell timing and aim

**RO DNA:** Fire Bolt / Lightning Bolt

---

## 2. Mana Shield

**Type:** Active – Defensive conversion
**Cooldown:** Medium (12s)

**Effect**

* Incoming damage partially drains mana instead of HP

**Why it exists**

* Caster sustain without healing spam
* Makes mana a defensive resource
* Allows skilled players to survive mistakes

**RO DNA:** Safety Wall energy

---

## 3. Frost Field

**Type:** Active – Area control
**Cooldown:** Medium–Long (14–16s)

**Effect**

* Creates icy zone on ground
* Enemies inside are slowed

**Why it exists**

* Space control instead of raw damage
* Enables kiting and setup
* Strong synergy with Arcane Bolt

**RO DNA:** Storm Gust philosophy (but toned down)

---

## 4. Arcane Drain

**Type:** Active – Sustain
**Cooldown:** Long (18s)

**Effect**

* Damages enemies in small area
* Restores mana per enemy hit

**Why it exists**

* Encourages fighting groups
* Prevents passive mana regen
* High risk, high reward positioning

**RO DNA:** Soul Drain / Sage identity

---

# Balance Snapshot (Why This Works)

| Class  | Sustain Source          | Control | Burst  | Mobility |
| ------ | ----------------------- | ------- | ------ | -------- |
| Melee  | Lifesteal via combat    | Medium  | Medium | Low      |
| Ranged | Spacing & DoT           | Low     | Medium | High     |
| Caster | Mana conversion & drain | High    | High   | Low      |

No class:

* Requires potions to function
* Has infinite sustain
* Can ignore positioning
